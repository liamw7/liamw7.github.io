The Project

In the age of big data and increasingly thorough analysis, the methods for evaluating a football player's performance remain largely qualitative. A player's performance is determined most commonly by pundits, football analysts and fans. There exist some quantitative methods, with fantasy football being the most famous, however there is no all-encapsulting, widely respected method that can conclusively determine a player's performance. This project aims to change that.

The dataset studied is rich and spans every event, within every match, of the 2013/14 Premier League season. This has been used to analyse the performance of every player. This radar graph is a snippet from the project and compares the stats of each player to the average of all those in his position.
